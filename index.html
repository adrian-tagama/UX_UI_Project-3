Stay on top of all the services you’re subscribed to.

WhatSub is a subscription management app that keeps you in control of all of your subscriptions and helps you save time and money!

Control at your fingertips.

Important subscription details at a glance


See your upcoming due dates, where your money is going, and each of the services you’ve subscribed to, organized into smart categories.

Find the latest deals on services you like.

Subscribe to services you like or try out some new ones


Check out the Explore page to easily find new services and promotions! You can review ratings to help you decide on them. Remember when we said we'd save you time and money?

Save subscriptions in your Wishlist


If you find something you like but aren't ready to commit just yet, save the subscription in your Wishlist!

Enjoy stress-free cancellations

Cancel a subscription in just a few taps


Say goodbye to long hold times with customer support and struggling to cancel a service you don’t need. WhatSub saves you time and money!

Schedule automatic cancellations


Thinking about cancelling a subscription, but aren't sure just yet? Throw it in the Wastebin! WhatSub will notify you before it cancels the service just in case you change your mind and decide to continue using the service.
