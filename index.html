Stay on top of all the services you’re subscribed to.

WhatSub is a subscription management app that keeps you in control of all of your subscriptions and helps you save time and money!

Control at your fingertips.

Important subscription details at a glance


See your upcoming due dates, where your money is going, and each of the services you’ve subscribed to, organized into smart categories.

Pay for monthly subscriptions, when you want.

Change the payment due date of a subscription


Do you need to schedule due dates to a different day of the month? Change that date with ease in just a few taps.

Bulk pay on one day


Are you tired of keeping track of when each subscription bill is due? Schedule your monthly subscriptions' payment due dates to a day of your choosing between the 1st and 27th or the last day of each month.

Enjoy stress-free cancellations

Cancel a subscription in just a few taps


Say goodbye to long hold times with customer support and struggling to cancel a service you don’t need. WhatSub saves you time and money!

Schedule automatic cancellations


Thinking about cancelling a subscription? Throw it in the Wastebin! WhatSub will notify you before it cancels the service just in case you change your mind and decide to continue using the service.
